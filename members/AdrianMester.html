A Touching Conversation

Spring 2019

Grace Liu

Carter Williams

Adrian Mester

Joseph Perrino

Dominic Calkosz

A conversation game about touch. After filling out a preliminary survey, players on either side of the wall place their hands into the box. They will then be prompted with charades style questions based on the comfort level inferred from the survey. Players must attempt to communicate complex ideas using only their hands.This game was created for Carson's Intro to Contextual Practice at CMU.

Backside Breakout

Fall 2018

Carter Williams

Tomas San Miguel

Adrian Mester

Tiger Jia

Dominic Calkosz

Backside Breakout is a prison break role-playing game. Gameplay is turn based with multiple turns a day before players have to move back to their cells and lose any items not properly hidden. The most convenient place to stash items is in one's rectum, hence the title Backside Breakout. The game is won by fulfilling one of three different escape routes, possibly involving bribery, disguises, or a copious amount of spoons. This game was created at the 2018 Pittsburgh IGDA Board Game Jam, for the theme: Freedom.
