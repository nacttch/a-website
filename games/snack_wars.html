Snack Wars

- Spring 2009

About

2D Run and Gun, based on snacks

Take control of Colonel Jiffy as he charges into war against the other snack foods. Work your way through three levels, featuring various enemies and guns to use, all based on your favorite foods. It keeps track of your best runs, so try to get a high score.
