Cubes and Lasers Bossmen consists of seven unique boss battles. You are a hero, equipped with a lasergun and jetpack to defeat the Bossmen. The game has a typographic health system, with parts of the characters’ names disappearing as damage is taken. An astute observer might ask, why are you fighting? Can’t we all get along? Well, being cubist, the Bossmen have taken a disliking to your supple round corners and high polygon count. And for Bossmen, disputes are not solved by words, but by lasers.

It's a race to claim the most blocks! A frantic race to see who can claim the most blocks within the time limit!
